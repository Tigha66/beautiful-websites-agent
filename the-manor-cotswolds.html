Our Heritage

A Historic Manor Reborn

Built in 1720, The Manor has been lovingly restored to become the Cotswolds' premier boutique hotel. Every detail honours our heritage while embracing contemporary luxury.

Set within 15 acres of award-winning gardens, The Manor offers a sanctuary from the modern world. Whether you're here for a romantic escape, a culinary adventure, or a wellness retreat, we promise an unforgettable experience.
