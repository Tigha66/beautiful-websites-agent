Our Philosophy

Changing Seasons 四季の変化

Yane (屋根) means "roof" in Japanese - a place of shelter and warmth. We bring authentic Japanese home cooking to Manchester, focusing on seasonal ingredients and traditional preparation methods.

Our menu changes with the seasons, featuring donburi rice bowls, katsu curry, and our signature homemade tsukemono pickles. Every dish is prepared with care and attention to detail, staying true to Japanese culinary traditions.

We're proud to be recognised as one of Manchester's most authentic Japanese restaurants, with 4.7/5 stars on TripAdvisor.
