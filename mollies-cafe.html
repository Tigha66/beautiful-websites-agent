Our Story

Welcome to Mollie's

Mollie's Cafe is a welcoming independent cafe in the heart of Wallingford, Oxfordshire. We pride ourselves on serving homemade food and speciality coffee in a relaxed, friendly atmosphere.

Our menu features all-day brunch, a selection of homemade cakes baked fresh daily, and quality coffee from local roasters. Whether you're meeting friends for coffee, enjoying a leisurely brunch, or grabbing a quick bite, our team is here to make you feel at home.

We're proud to be a favourite among locals and visitors alike, with 4.5/5 stars on TripAdvisor.
