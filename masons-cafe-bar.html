Our Story

Family Run Since 2015

Mason's Cafe Bar is a small family run business in the heart of Worsley. We pride ourselves on serving homemade food in a contemporary, welcoming setting.

Our menu features all-day brunch, speciality coffee, and a selection of lunch and tapas options. Everything is made fresh on-site using quality, local ingredients.

Whether you're meeting friends for coffee, enjoying a leisurely brunch, or grabbing a quick lunch, our team is here to make you feel at home.
