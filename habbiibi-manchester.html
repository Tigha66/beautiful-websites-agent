Our Story

Where Dubai Meets the Mediterranean

Habbiibi brings the vibrant energy of Dubai's culinary scene to Manchester. Our name, meaning "my love" in Arabic, reflects our passion for food and hospitality.

Founded by chefs with experience in Michelin-starred kitchens across the Mediterranean, we blend traditional recipes with modern techniques. Every dish is a celebration of flavour, culture, and the art of bringing people together.

From our wood-fired grill to our house-made pastas, we're committed to authenticity and excellence. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience.
