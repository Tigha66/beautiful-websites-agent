Frequently Asked Questions

Are the calculators really free?

Yes! All calculators on IslamicCalc are 100% free to use. We sustain the platform through optional donations and ethical advertising.

Are the calculations Sharia-compliant?

Our calculators are built based on widely-accepted Islamic finance principles. However, interpretations may vary between scholars. We recommend verifying important calculations with your trusted Islamic finance advisor.

Do you store my financial data?

No! All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or have access to any financial data you enter.

Can I partner with IslamicCalc?

Yes! We welcome partnerships with Islamic banks, financial institutions, and Sharia-compliant businesses. Contact us at partners@islamiccalc.com to discuss opportunities.
