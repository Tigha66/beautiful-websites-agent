Our Story

Serving Since 2015

The Chippy brings traditional British fish & chips to your neighbourhood. We use only sustainably sourced cod from UK waters, hand-cut chips from local potatoes, and our secret batter recipe that's been perfected over years.

Every order is cooked fresh to order in our traditional fryers. Whether you're dining in or taking away, you'll get hot, crispy, delicious fish & chips every time.
