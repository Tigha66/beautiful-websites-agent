Our Story

Homemade Punjabi Cuisine

Caraway Cafe brings authentic vegetarian Indian cuisine to Gerrards Cross. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, using traditional spices and cooking methods.

We're proud to be 100% vegetarian, offering a wide range of dishes from mild kormas to spicy vindaloos. Whether you're dining in or ordering takeaway, every dish is prepared fresh to order with the finest ingredients.

We also offer catering services for parties, events, and corporate functions. Call us to discuss your requirements.
