Our Story

Authentic Indian Since 2010

Spice Garden brings authentic Indian cuisine to your neighbourhood. Our chefs use traditional recipes passed down through generations, with fresh spices ground daily.

From our famous tikka masala to our fragrant biryanis, every dish is prepared with care and attention to detail. We source the finest ingredients and import many spices directly from India.

Whether you prefer mild korma or spicy vindaloo, we have something for every taste. All our dishes can be made vegetarian, vegan, or gluten-free on request.
