Our Story

Authentic Italian Since 2015

Pizza Palace brings authentic Italian pizza to your neighbourhood. Our dough is made fresh daily using Italian 00 flour and fermented for 48 hours for that perfect crispy-chewy texture.

We import our San Marzano tomatoes from Naples, our mozzarella di bufala from Campania, and our olive oil from Puglia. Every ingredient tells a story of Italian tradition.

Our wood-fired oven reaches 450°C, cooking pizzas in just 90 seconds for that authentic charred crust and melted cheese perfection.
