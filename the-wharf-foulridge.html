Our Venue

Canal-Side Dining & Drinks

The Wharf at Foulridge is a stunning venue located alongside the Leeds & Liverpool Canal. We offer a unique setting for dining, drinks, and events with beautiful canal views.

Whether you're looking for a family-friendly pub, a venue for live music, or a picturesque spot for a Sunday roast, The Wharf has something for everyone. Our outdoor seating area is perfect for sunny days by the canal.

We host regular events including live music nights, quiz nights, and special celebrations. Check our social media for upcoming events.
