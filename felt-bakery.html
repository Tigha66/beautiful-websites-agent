Our Story

Baked with Love in Wimbledon

Felt Bakery began with a simple mission: to bring authentic, handcrafted baked goods to the Wimbledon community. Every morning at 4am, we start fresh — mixing, kneading, and baking with traditional techniques passed down through generations.

We source our flour from local mills, our butter from British dairies, and our ingredients from trusted suppliers who share our commitment to quality. The result? Pastries that melt in your mouth and bread that makes every meal special.
